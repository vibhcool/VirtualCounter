The official website to book tickets to railway platforms.


Users are requested to :

  • 1. Sign-up/Log-in
  • 2. Enter Number of tickets required, Platform number, Date and Time of Entry and Duration of stay at the platform.
  • 3. Take a screenshot of generated E-Ticket and keep it with you at all times.
  • 4. Don't delete the screenshot at least till you leave the platform.
